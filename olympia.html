Olympia

Where Nature Meets Government

Olympia, the capital city of Washington State, is located in the South Puget Sound region. As of the 2020 census, it had a population of 55,605. The city was officially incorporated on January 28, 1859. Classified as an urban area, Olympia serves as the county seat of Thurston County and is a central hub for government and culture in the region. The median household income in Olympia is $76,930, which is slightly below the Washington state median of $84,247.
